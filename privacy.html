Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your data

Last Updated: January 26, 2026
Our Commitment: We believe privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We're committed to transparency and protecting your information.

What We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use reFrame, you provide:

  • Messages: The text you enter to be reframed
  • Conversation context (optional): Additional background information you choose to provide
  • Relationship type: Your selection of who you're communicating with (e.g., Partner, Manager, Family)
  • Email address (optional): If you sign up for updates or create an account

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • Device information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • IP address: For security and analytics purposes
  • R³ Feedback: Your selections when rating reframes (e.g., "This feels right" or "Try again")

How We Use Your Data

To Provide the Service

  • Process your messages through our AI to generate reframes
  • Analyze messages for toxic communication patterns using RFD™ (see below)
  • Provide educational warnings when harmful patterns are detected
  • Improve the quality and accuracy of reframes
  • Send you product updates (if you opt in)

To Improve reFrame

  • Analyze usage patterns to understand what features are most helpful
  • Improve RFD™ detection accuracy based on aggregated patterns
  • Identify technical issues and bugs
  • Develop new features based on user needs
Important: We do NOT store your personal messages long-term. Messages are processed to provide the reframe and RFD™ analysis, then deleted. We may retain anonymized, aggregated data for product improvement (e.g., "Users reframe messages about workplace relationships 40% of the time" or "Criticism patterns were detected in 15% of messages") but never individual messages tied to you.

RFD™ Red Flag Detection Technology

What is RFD™?

RFD™ (Red Flag Detection) is our proprietary technology that analyzes your message for toxic communication patterns before you send it. Built on 40+ years of Gottman Institute research, RFD™ detects patterns like criticism, contempt, defensiveness, stonewalling, gaslighting, manipulation, and threats.

How RFD™ Works

When you submit a message to reFrame:

  1. Your message is analyzed by our AI to detect harmful communication patterns
  2. If toxic patterns are found, you receive an educational warning explaining why the pattern is harmful
  3. You choose whether to edit your message or proceed to reframing
  4. The analysis is then deleted—we do not store RFD™ results tied to you

What RFD™ Is NOT

It's important to understand what RFD™ does not do:

  • NOT a diagnosis: RFD™ identifies communication patterns, not mental health conditions
  • NOT therapy: RFD™ is educational, not a substitute for professional counseling
  • NOT judgment: RFD™ doesn't evaluate you as a person—only specific communication patterns
  • NOT blocking: You always maintain full control. RFD™ informs, you decide
  • NOT stored: RFD™ analysis is not saved or tracked at an individual level

RFD™ Data Collection

For RFD™ specifically, we collect anonymized, aggregated data only:

  • Which patterns are most commonly detected (e.g., "Criticism: 20%, Contempt: 5%")
  • What severity levels are triggered (high, medium, low)
  • Whether users choose to edit or proceed after warnings
  • General accuracy metrics to improve detection

We do NOT collect:

  • Your actual message content for RFD™ analysis storage
  • Individual pattern histories tied to you
  • Information about specific relationships or situations

What We DON'T Do

We will NEVER:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your messages with anyone without your consent
  • Use your messages for advertising purposes
  • Train AI models on your personal conversations without permission
  • Share your information with social media platforms
  • Store RFD™ warnings or patterns tied to your identity
  • Use RFD™ data to make judgments about your relationships or mental health

Data Security

However: No system is 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Use reFrame with the understanding that any data transmitted online carries some risk.
