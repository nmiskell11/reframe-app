Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your data

Last Updated: January 2026
Our Commitment: We believe privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We're committed to transparency and protecting your information.

What We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use reFrame, you provide:

  • Messages: The text you enter to be reframed
  • Email address (optional): If you sign up for updates or create an account

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • Device information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • IP address: For security and analytics purposes

How We Use Your Data

To Provide the Service

  • Process your messages through our AI to generate reframes
  • Improve the quality of reframes
  • Send you product updates (if you opt in)

To Improve reFrame

  • Analyze usage patterns to understand what features are most helpful
  • Identify technical issues and bugs
  • Develop new features based on user needs
Important: We do NOT store your personal messages long-term. Messages are processed to provide the reframe and then deleted. We may retain anonymized, aggregated data for product improvement (e.g., "Users reframe messages about relationships 60% of the time") but never individual messages tied to you.

What We DON'T Do

We will NEVER:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your messages with anyone without your consent
  • Use your messages for advertising purposes
  • Train AI models on your personal conversations without permission
  • Share your information with social media platforms

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

Anthropic (Claude AI)

Your messages are processed through Anthropic's API to generate reframes. Anthropic has strict privacy policies and does not use API data to train their models. Learn more at anthropic.com/privacy.

Netlify (Hosting)

Our website is hosted on Netlify's secure infrastructure. They handle basic website operations but do not have access to your messages.

Google Analytics (Optional)

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use our site (e.g., which pages are most visited). This data is anonymized. You can opt out using browser extensions like uBlock Origin.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of any data we have about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Opt out: Unsubscribe from emails or stop using the service
  • Correct: Update any inaccurate information
  • Port: Request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@wereframe.com.

Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • Access to data is limited to essential personnel only
  • We regularly review and update our security measures
However: No system is 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Use reFrame with the understanding that any data transmitted online carries some risk.

Children's Privacy

reFrame is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

International Users

reFrame is operated from the United States. If you're using reFrame from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. By using reFrame, you consent to this transfer.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function (e.g., remembering your session)
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand site usage (optional, can be blocked)

We do NOT use advertising or tracking cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll notify you via:

  • A notice on our website
  • Email (if you've provided one)

Continued use of reFrame after changes means you accept the updated policy.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you're a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt out of data sales (note: we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you're in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data:

Email: hello@wereframe.com

Website: wereframe.com

Our Promise: We're building reFrame to help people communicate better, not to exploit their data. Your privacy matters to us. If you ever have concerns, please reach out. We're here to help.
